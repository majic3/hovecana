About

Cascaded StyleSheet for horisontal and vertical navigation menus or tabs. Not depended on content ("Uordered List" tag only)

Stylesheet


.ver ul {right:-100%;top:0}
.nav ul{visibility:hidden;display:none}

.nav li:hover>ul,.nav li:hover{visibility:visible}
.nav li:hover>.hor,.hor li{display:inline-block}
.nav li:hover>.ver,.ver li{display:block}

ul.nav,.hor ul>li,.ver ul>li{position:relative}
.nav ul.hor, .nav ul.ver{position:absolute}
